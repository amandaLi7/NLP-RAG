A Tartan is a twilled woolen fabric with a plaid design, often misrepresented as a fierce warrior from either the Asian tundra or Scottish highlands. In actuality, a Tartan is a check or pattern in a variety of colors in woven fabric in which bands of color are repeated in equal proportion in warp (running lengthwise) and weft (running across). It is of Scottish origin and consists of stripes of various colors and widths against a solid ground, denoting a particular family lineage.
